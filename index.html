Biography

I'm from Washington state but I've lived in a few other countries before settling down most recently in West Seattle. My twin brother and his wife brought my amazing niece into the world, and I enjoy spending time with my family when I can. I have a lot of hobbies like gardening, cooking, mushroom hunting, and experimenting with fermentation. A couple interesting things to come out of this time of isolation are a funky kimchi ferment and turning my shell collection into a windchime. Oh ya, and I signed up for a school to get going on the rest of my life!
